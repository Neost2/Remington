## Part 2: Team Processes

### 1. Communication

**How the team will communicate (frequency and methods):**

- Zoom, text, and email
- Quick check-ins 2-3 times per week
- Weekly progress sync meeting

**Maximum expected response time:**

- 24 hours for normal messages
- Same day for urgent blocker messages

### 2. Decision-Making

**How decisions will be made in this team / how you will stay on track:**

- Case-by-case review with team input
- Final project-level calls made by Desmond after team suggestions
- Set deadlines, track progress weekly, and lock milestones

### 3. Conflict Resolution

**How will you resolve differences?**

- Talk issues through directly and respectfully
- Revisit shared goals and role commitments
- Escalate unresolved items to a full-team discussion

### 4. Commitments

**How will you handle different levels of participation and commitment?**

- Work with each other and actively support teammates
- Raise concerns early when workload or timelines are at risk
- Reassign tasks if needed to keep delivery on track

**Process if someone does not meet responsibilities:**

1. Private check-in to understand the blocker
2. Agree on a recovery plan and new deadline
3. Rebalance tasks if needed

**Consequences for poor performance:**

- Reduced ownership on critical-path tasks
- Additional check-ins and progress accountability

### 5. Other

**Additional commitments/goals/processes:**

- Keep communicating consistently
- Maintain shared visibility of deadlines and responsibilities
- Support each other to complete the platform successfully
