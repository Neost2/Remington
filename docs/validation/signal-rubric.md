# CarePath Validation Signal Rubric

Use this rubric to score each conversation consistently.

## Weak

- Problem is mentioned but vague
- Limited urgency or low consequence
- No clear adoption intent
- Feedback is mostly hypothetical

## Medium

- Problem is specific and real
- Impact is clear in at least one dimension (time, stress, cost, health)
- Adoption intent is uncertain ("maybe" or conditional)
- Some feature-value alignment is present

## Strong

- Problem is concrete, recurring, and high-impact
- Consequences are explicit (for example: missed care, workflow breakdown, avoidable cost)
- Participant expresses clear intent to use or support use
- Requested capabilities align directly with CarePath concept

## Evidence Checklist

For each conversation, ensure you captured:

- At least 1 direct quote
- 1 specific incident or example
- 1 impact statement (logistical, medical, emotional, or financial)
- 1 adoption signal (Yes/Maybe/No + why)
- 1 top requested feature and 1 top concern
